About Us

At Bison Alliance, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the way real estate agents connect with potential clients. Our journey began when our CEO, while serving as a Sales Manager at a prospecting company, identified a significant gap in the industry: real estate agents were often subjected to miscommunications, offered subpar services, and burdened with upfront deposits and hefty referral fees for leads that seldom translated into valuable opportunities.

Motivated to address these challenges, our CEO collaborated with like-minded team members to create a solution that redefines cold calling and lead generation. With nearly a decade of hands-on experience in cold calling, real estate lead generation, and a proven track record of assisting agents in closing over $1.5 billion in property transactions, our leadership team possesses unparalleled expertise.

Our CEO's extensive background in customer service and leadership of high-performing sales teams ensures that our approach is both client-centric and results-driven. Each member of the Bison Alliance team is a qualified professional, committed to delivering exceptional service and fostering genuine partnerships with real estate agents.
